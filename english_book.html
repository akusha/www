A day in the lefe of Simon Carrot

First, I want to tell you about myself. I am thirteen and I am in the third form at school in Manchester. I have a little sister Bess who is very silly and I have a very clever dog. His name is Pal

What a bad day Monday was for me! I woke up late and did not have time to eat me breakfast. I did not see my books and my school cap in their places. I looked in my room. I looked in the dining-room. Then I looked into the bathroom and saw my books there. Then I saw my dog Pal under the table with my cap. I put my books into my bag, took my cap and ran very quicly to schol.

The first lesson on Monday is maths. What a difficult subject to begin the week with! Mr.Green, the mathematics teacher, took our homework. He saw that my exercise book was dirty. He given me a bad mark for it. Then he gave us tasks to do. I sit behind Jane, who is fat and silly. But she likes maths very much and has good narks for it. I asked her to tell me the answers, but she didn't want to.

The second lesson on Monday is French. I like Franch. It's nice to talk to yuor frends at the lesson in another language. Then it was eleven o'clock - time for break. Nell gave me a piece of cake to eat with my milk and then gave me her homework to copy. I think Nell likes me, and she is a nice girl.

After break we had two lesson in Latin! They are bad lessons. I do not like Latin.
